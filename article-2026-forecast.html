Home Optimization Tips for 2026

1. Activate Your Entrance

Your front door is the "mouth of Chi." In 2026:

  • Paint your door red or add red accents
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Keep the entrance clear and welcoming
  • Add a welcome mat with auspicious symbols

2. Optimize Your Bedroom

For rest and rejuvenation:

  • Position bed with headboard against a solid wall
  • Avoid mirrors facing the bed
  • Use calming earth tones (beige, cream, light pink)
  • Remove electronics or cover them at night

3. Energize Your Living Room

Create a vibrant gathering space:

  • Add red or orange throw pillows
  • Display family photos in the Southwest
  • Use round or oval coffee tables (promotes harmony)
  • Ensure good natural light

4. Enhance Your Kitchen

The kitchen represents nourishment and abundance:

  • Keep stove clean and in good working order
  • Store knives out of sight
  • Add fresh fruit in a bowl (especially oranges)
  • Use warm lighting
