‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Where should I place a feng shui fountain for wealth?
The best locations are the North (career), Southeast (wealth), and East (health/family) sectors of your home. Near the entrance is also excellent as it attracts wealth chi into the home. Never place in the South, bedroom, or kitchen.
How many fish should be in a feng shui aquarium?
The classic feng shui number is 9 fish: 8 goldfish (gold represents wealth) plus 1 black fish (absorbs negative energy). If 9 is too many, use 6 (smooth sailing) or 8 (prosperity). Always use odd total numbers for yang energy.
Is a water fountain good in the bedroom?
No! Water in the bedroom is a major feng shui taboo. It creates excessive yin energy, disrupts sleep, can cause relationship issues, and may lead to financial instability. Keep all water features out of bedrooms.
Should fountain water flow towards or away from the house?
Water should always flow TOWARDS the house or INWARD toward the home's center. This symbolizes wealth flowing into your life. Water flowing outward or away from the house represents money leaving.
Can I use an image of water instead of real water?
Images of calm water (lakes, gentle oceans) can activate minor water energy, but real moving water is far more powerful. Avoid images of storms, crashing waves, or waterfalls falling away from you.
