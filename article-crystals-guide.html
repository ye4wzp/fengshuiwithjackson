‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best crystal for wealth in feng shui?
Citrine is considered the premier wealth crystal in feng shui. Known as the "Merchant's Stone," it attracts prosperity and success. Place citrine in your wealth corner (Southeast) or on your desk. Pyrite ("Fool's Gold") and green jade are also excellent for wealth.
Where should I place feng shui crystals in my home?
Place crystals based on their purpose: Wealth crystals (citrine, pyrite) in the Southeast wealth corner. Love crystals (rose quartz) in the Southwest love corner or bedroom. Protection crystals (black obsidian) near the front door. Health crystals in the center or East sector.
How do I cleanse and charge feng shui crystals?
Cleanse crystals monthly using these methods: 1) Running water (not for porous stones), 2) Moonlight overnight (especially full moon), 3) Sunlight for a few hours (not for amethyst/rose quartz which fade), 4) Sage smoke, 5) Burying in sea salt. Charge by setting intentions while holding the crystal.
What crystals should not be placed in the bedroom?
Avoid high-energy crystals in the bedroom as they can disrupt sleep: clear quartz (too activating), tiger's eye (too yang), citrine near the bed (too stimulating). Rose quartz, amethyst, and moonstone are ideal bedroom crystals for their calming, yin energy.
