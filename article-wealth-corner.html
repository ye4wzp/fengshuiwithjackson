Many people think Feng Shui is complicated math. While traditional schools use compass directions, looking for the "Xun" (Wealth) sector in a modern apartment is actually quite simple.

In this guide, I will show you how to locate the Wealth Corner in your home instantly and, more importantly, how to activate it to start shifting your financial energy.

Step 1: The "Front Door" Method

The easiest way to apply the Bagua Map to your home is by using your front door as the anchor.

üìê Simple Steps:
  1. Stand at your front entrance, facing into your house
  2. Imagine a Tic-Tac-Toe grid (9 squares) overlaying your entire floor plan
  3. Look to the far left corner of your home
  4. That is your Wealth Corner

Note: You can also apply this to a single room. Stand at the bedroom door; the far left corner is the wealth spot of that room.

Step 2: What Should Be Here? (The Activators)

Now that you've found it, look at what's currently there. Is it a trash can? A pile of laundry? A dead plant?

If your Wealth Corner is cluttered, your finances will feel "blocked." To activate flow, we introduce elements that represent growth (Wood) and flow (Water).

Here are my Top 3 Wealth Activators:

1. Living Plants (Wood Energy)

Wood represents upward growth. A healthy, vibrant plant is the best anchor for this corner. Avoid cacti (spikes create conflict).

Top Pick: The "Money Tree" (Pachira Aquatica) or a Rubber Plant with round leaves.

Step 3: What If My Wealth Corner is a Bathroom?

Don't panic. This is a common layout. It just means you need to be mindful that wealth energy doesn't "flush away."

üöΩ The Cure:
  • Keep the toilet lid closed at all times
  • Keep the drains plugged when not in use
  • Add a vertically growing plant (like Bamboo) to push the energy back up
  • Use Earth element colors (beige, terracotta) to ground the space
