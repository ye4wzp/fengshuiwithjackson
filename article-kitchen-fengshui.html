Stove Placement: The Heart of Kitchen Feng Shui

The stove is the most important element in kitchen feng shui. It represents your family's wealth and the cook's authority.

Command Position for Stove

  • ‚úÖ Cook should face the entrance while cooking
  • ‚úÖ Stove should have a solid wall behind it
  • ‚úÖ Good ventilation above the stove
  • ‚úÖ Adequate space around the stove

What to Avoid

  • ‚ùå Stove directly facing the front door
  • ‚ùå Stove under a window (unstable energy)
  • ‚ùå Stove with your back to the entrance
  • ‚ùå Stove sharing a wall with a toilet

  • Use all burners regularly (rotate usage)

The stove represents Fire (wealth creation), while the sink represents Water (money flow). These elements can clash if not properly balanced.

Ideal Arrangement

  • Green dish towel
