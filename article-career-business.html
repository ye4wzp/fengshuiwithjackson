‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the career sector in feng shui?
The career sector is located in the NORTH area of your home or office. It's governed by the Water element. Activate it with water features, images of water, blue/black colors, and career-related symbols to boost professional opportunities.
How should I position my desk for career success?
Position your desk in the "command position" - where you can see the door but aren't directly in line with it. Have a solid wall behind you for support. Avoid sitting with your back to the door or facing a wall, which blocks opportunities and vision.
What feng shui symbols attract business success?
Key business success symbols include: Dragon (power and authority), Dragon Tortoise (career advancement), Sailing Ship (bringing in wealth), Ru Yi (authority scepter), Crystal Globe (expanding business reach), and Three-Legged Toad (attracting money).
Can feng shui help me get a promotion?
Yes! Focus on these areas: 1) Activate the North (career sector) with water element, 2) Enhance the NW for mentor luck and helpful people, 3) Place a Dragon Tortoise on your desk facing outward, 4) Ensure good command position at work, 5) Remove clutter to allow chi flow.
