‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Where should I place my sofa for good feng shui?

Your sofa should be in the command position with its back against a solid wall, facing the entrance but not directly in line with the door. A diagonal view of the entrance is ideal. This position provides support, stability, and allows you to see opportunities coming.

What shape coffee table is best for feng shui?

Round or oval coffee tables are best for feng shui because they have no sharp corners (avoiding "poison arrows"), promote harmony and flow, and encourage equal participation in conversations. They're also safer for children.

Where is the wealth corner in my living room?

Stand at your living room entrance facing inward. The far left corner is your wealth area. Activate it by placing healthy plants (money tree, jade plant), adding purple or green items, keeping it clean and well-lit, and optionally adding a small water feature or crystals.

What colors are best for living room feng shui?

For wealth: green, purple, and gold accents. For harmony: beige, cream, soft pink, and peach. For energy: yellow, orange, and red accents (use sparingly). Avoid all-black, dark blue, or too much white as they can create heavy or sterile energy.

Can I have a TV in my living room according to feng shui?

Yes, but place it on a solid wall (not floating), avoid placing it opposite the entrance, and ideally position it on the East or Southeast wall. When off, the black screen acts like a mirror, so cover it with a decorative cloth or use a TV cabinet with doors to minimize negative energy.
