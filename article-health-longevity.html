‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the health sector in feng shui?
The East sector of your home governs health and family in feng shui. This is the Wood element sector, best activated with healthy plants, the color green, and images of sunrise, forests, or vibrant nature scenes.
What is the Wu Lou gourd in feng shui?
The Wu Lou (Ëë´Ëä¶) is a bottle gourd shape used as a powerful health cure in Chinese tradition. It's believed to absorb negative energy and illness chi. Place brass Wu Lou by the bedside of anyone recovering from illness or for general health protection.
Can feng shui help with chronic illness?
Feng shui supports healing by optimizing the energy environment, but should complement (not replace) medical treatment. Key steps include clearing clutter, ensuring good air and light, removing sha chi (negative energy sources), and activating the East health sector.
What colors promote health in feng shui?
Green is the primary health color (Wood element). Light blue supports healing and calm. Earth tones (beige, terracotta) provide stability during recovery. Avoid excessive red (too stimulating) or black (too yin) in spaces for the unwell.
Where should a sick person sleep for better feng shui?
Position the bed so the sick person can see the door (command position), with head pointing toward their personal health direction or East. Ensure good fresh air, avoid beams overhead, and place Wu Lou or healing crystals nearby.
